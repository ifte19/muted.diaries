About Me

Hi, I’m Iftekar Uddin, a 23-year-old photography enthusiast and third-year student at CUET. Photography for me is more than capturing images — it is emotion, light, silence, and storytelling.

I started my journey with mobile photography, discovering beauty in everyday moments. Muted Diaries is my visual diary — where street life, abstract forms, and nature's calm atmosphere meet. Every frame reflects how I see the world: quiet, raw, and meaningful.
